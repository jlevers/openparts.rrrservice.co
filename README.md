# OpenParts

**Open-source mechanical parts database. Repair, Resilience, Regeneration.**

https://openparts.rrrservice.co

## What is this?

A community-driven database of part numbers, cross-references, dimensions, and rebuild guides for mechanical equipment — tractors, cars, motorcycles, heavy equipment, anything with parts that wear out and need replacing.

Built because mechanics shouldn't have to dig through 50 websites to find a part number, and that knowledge shouldn't disappear when a dealer closes or a forum goes offline.

## Three tenets

- **Repair** — Fix it, don't trash it
- **Resilience** — Build knowledge that outlasts any single dealer or parts house
- **Regeneration** — Keep machines running, keep stuff out of landfills

## Contributing

See [Contributing Guide](https://openparts.rrrservice.co/contributing/) on the site, or just fork [jlevers/openparts.rrrservice.co](https://github.com/jlevers/openparts.rrrservice.co), add/edit markdown files, and open a PR.

Good contributions include:
- Part numbers and cross-references
- Dimensions (OD, ID, thickness, spline count, thread pitch)
- Rebuild vs. replace guidance
- Supplier info and pricing
- Corrections to existing data

## Tech
